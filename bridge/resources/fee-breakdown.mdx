## Protocol Fee

The protocol fee is a flat 30 basis points (0.30%) applied on the source token amount. This fee is independent of network gas fees and does not fluctuate.

## Onramp Provider Fee

A fee is applied by the third-party provider during onramping. The fee amount is set by the provider and is typically calculated as a percentage of the total amount being onramped. Fees may vary depending on the specific provider and the selected payment method.

The protocol itself does not charge any additional fees for onramp transactions.

[Learn more about Onramp Providers.](/bridge/resources/onramp-providers)
